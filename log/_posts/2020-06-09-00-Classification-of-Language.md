### Classifying Languages

I started to classify Languages and found that the result matched what had already been done in the Language of Mathematical Groups. This is seen by thinking of Languages to emerge in pairs (element and its inverse to form identity). Thus, Languages can be classified corresponding to the classification of finite simple groups.
