Here's a bunch of links to articles related to BCS Superconductivity:
* [Wikipedia's Introductory article on Cooper Pairs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cooper_pair)
* [Why Cooper pairing is not enough](https://www.quora.com/How-does-the-Cooper-pairing-mechanism-give-rise-to-zero-electrical-resistance): This quora answer explains why coherence is needed to form a condensate. Standard references like Tinkham's Intro to Superconductivity are also mentioned.
