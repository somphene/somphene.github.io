### Idempotent 

* Last time we defined nilpotent. Now we explore a related concept, which is $$\textrm{Idempotent}$$: An element $$e$$ such that $$e^2 =e$$ holds.

### Split-Semisimple Commutative Algebras
* Commutative $$\mathbb{K}$$-Algebra that is isomorphic as an algebra to a product of copies of $$\mathbb{K}$$.
* It does not contain any non-zero nilpotent elements.
