### Classifying Physics Languages

Based on [Classifying Languages](https://somphene.github.io/2020-06-09-00-Classification-of-Language/), a special case will be Classifying Physics Languages. This will be done starting with Identity Language. Next, I will generate Newton's Laws of motion as a composition of complementary pairs to form a cyclic Language. This will be followed by Thermodynamcis and Relativity. Quantum Mechanics and Quantum Gravity will also be generated. This will complete the cycle of currently known languages in Physics.
