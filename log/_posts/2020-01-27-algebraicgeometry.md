### Course Overview
* [Prof. Dipendra Prasad](http://www.math.iitb.ac.in/~dprasad/) is taking this course at an advanced graduate level. He taught Differential Topology last sem which followed Victor Guilleman and Pollack's book. That background will help but is not necessary for this course.
* References are the standard- [David Mumford's The Red Book of Varieties and Schemes](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9783540632931) and for background in Commutative Algebra, refer to [Atiyah and Macdonald's Introduction to Commutative Algebra](https://www.amazon.in/Introduction-Commutative-Algebra-Atiyah-MacDonald/dp/8187169877) 
* Ring (taken to be commutative by default), Ideal Theory will be used heavily.
