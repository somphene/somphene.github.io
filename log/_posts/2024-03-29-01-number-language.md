### Language of Mathematics

### Abstract
 The language of Mathematics grew from the representation of objects such as an apple. Counting grew to construction of natural numbers. At some point, a higher notion of a variable was invented. A variable is now taken to be part of the standard language of mathematics including Algebra and Logic.
