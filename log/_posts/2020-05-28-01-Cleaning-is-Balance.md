### Cleaning

Everything has its space. Cleaning is an act of giving everything its place. Through Cleaning, we connect the objects with their appropriate place. It is similar to bringing back everyone to their home. While the pandemic has caused significant difficulties, grief and pain, there are some silver linings to look at. The pandemic has forced us to not just be clean individually but also brought many people back to their homes. This itself is an act of cleaning at the scale of the globe. Cleaning does not isolate the dust from that which is pure (not dust), but instead, it temporarily gives the dust its own space to settle. Cleaning identifies the place of dust to be going away from light towards the other end of the spectrum for some time, only to spring back and eventually come back to light in a good form. That is, Cleaning temporarily lets the dust go to its own place and eventually allows it to come back in a renewed form. Thus Cleaning is a Balancing act. Cleaning balances the right amount of things to keep in light and the left amount is sent to the dark to rest for a while.
