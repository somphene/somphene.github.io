### Communication

As I keep repeating, If given another opportunity, I would have done a bachelors degree in communication. As a result I feel the need to explicitly ask Institute to expand its horizons and let Communication be a major which students may pursue. Learning how to communicate or translate from one Language to the another is fundamental to our lives. Smooth translation is what makes us live together in a harmony. Multiple Languages are to be seen as parts which come together to form a whole. Complementing each other's Language is what brings universality in Communication.
