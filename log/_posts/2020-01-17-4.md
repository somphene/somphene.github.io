In Intro to Machine Learning (course webpage- https://www.cse.iitb.ac.in/~pjyothi/cs419/index.html) second class, Prof. [Jyothi](https://www.cse.iitb.ac.in/~pjyothi/) started with Linear model for regression. Error function was defined, absolute error and least squares error were introduced. Analytic closed form optimal solution for the least square error was calculated and shown to be the orthogonal projection onto the subspace spanned by the parameters. We also touched on fitting uding generic basis. There was no discussion of fourier theory or other analysis of convergnce as everything was finite dimensional. When I asked what about infinite dimensions, she said that this would naturally lead to kernel based methods. Completeness of basis was not at all doscussed.
