Some people to watch out for and follow in the field of Computational Materials Science, DFT and Materials Informatics are :

* [Kristin Persson](https://perssongroup.lbl.gov/): The Materials Project, Ab inito Computational methods and high-performance computing technology related work. 
* [Anubhav Jain](https://computron.github.io/): Computational design of materials, data analysis.
