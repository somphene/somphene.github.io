 - Kolmogorov Axiom: Experiment Model is Probability Space.
   - Probabibility Space $$(\Omega, \mathcal{F}, P )$$
    - Sample Space $$\Omega$$, Outcome Set
    - Event Space $$\mathcal{F}$$, $$\sigma$$-Algebra
    - Probabilty measure $$P \colon \mathcal{F} \to [0-1] $$.
